\chapter*{Declaration on the Use of Generative Artificial Intelligence}

I declare that the following generative AI tools were used during the preparation of this thesis in the ways stated below:

\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{ChatGPT (OpenAI)} was used in the period \textbf{August 2024--April 2026}.
  It was used for brainstorming and refining the thesis scope and structure, and for AI-assisted drafting and iterative revision of the written text (e.g., proposing phrasing, reorganizing paragraphs, and improving clarity and readability) across many parts of the thesis. It was also used for discussion of implementation options and trade-offs. All generated outputs were reviewed and edited by the author.

  \item \textbf{GitHub Copilot (GitHub/Microsoft)} was used in the period \textbf{August 2025--April 2026}.
  It was used primarily as code completion and assistance with repetitive implementation patterns. The final code and all technical decisions were authored, reviewed, and validated by the author.

  \item \textbf{Cursor (AI-assisted IDE)} was used in the period \textbf{December 2025--April 2026}.
  It was used to accelerate refactoring and repetitive edits (e.g., propagating interface changes across modules and updating related boilerplate). The author reviewed, corrected, and integrated all resulting changes.
\end{itemize}

After using the tools listed above, I thoroughly reviewed and modified the outputs as needed and I fully accept responsibility for the final content of the thesis, including all technical claims, experimental results, and interpretations.
