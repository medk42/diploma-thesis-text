# Style Prompt

Write in the style of Chapters 2 to 5 of this thesis.

Requirements:
- Use clear, technical prose with explicit rationale.
- Keep section openings purposeful.
- Prefer direct statements, careful tradeoffs, and bounded claims.
- Keep vocabulary stable: use case, module, control plane, workflow, scene detection, execution, persistence.
- Avoid hype, sales language, and generic AI-sounding phrasing.
- When discussing a result, explain what it means and why it matters.
- When discussing a limitation, state its practical consequence.
- Keep the tone calm, precise, and thesis-like.
- Use short connecting phrases like `However`, `At the same time`, `Therefore`, and `In practice` naturally, not mechanically.

If useful, structure paragraphs as:
1. purpose or context,
2. concrete observation or choice,
3. implication or limitation,
4. short bridge to the next point.
